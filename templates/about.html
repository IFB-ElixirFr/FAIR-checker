About us

#}
In this work we propose a web demonstrator, aimed at i) evaluating FAIR maturity indicators and ii) providing hints to progress in the FAIRification process.

This web page relies on FAIRMetrics implementation and the online APIs. We are grateful to Mark D. Wilkinson for is technical support on using the APIs.

FAIR-checker is funded and developed by the French Institute for Bioinformatics (Elixir-France). Source code and technical issue are accessible through GitHub: https://github.com/IFB-ElixirFr/fair-checker.git

Main developers are Thomas Rosnet (thomas.rosnet /at/ france-bioinformatique.fr) and Alban Gaignard (alban.gaignard /at/ univ-nantes.fr). Feel free to contact us !
