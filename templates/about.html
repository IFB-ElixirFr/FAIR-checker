About us

The team

FAIR-checker is developed by the interoperability working group of the French Institute for Bioinformatics :
  • Thomas Rosnet (thomas.rosnet@france-bioinformatique.fr)
  • Alban Gaignard (alban.gaignard@univ-nantes.fr)
  • Marie-Dominique Devignes (marie-dominique.devignes@loria.fr)
The source code is accessible through GitHub https://github.com/IFB-ElixirFr/fair-checker.git. Please submit GitHub issues to provide feedback or ask for new features. and contact us for any related question.

Acknowledments
